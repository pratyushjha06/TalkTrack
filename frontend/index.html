About TalkTrack

TalkTrack is an AI-powered meeting assistant designed to streamline your meetings. With automatic audio and video recording, real-time transcription, and intelligent summarization, TalkTrack ensures that you never miss important details during any conversation. Whether you’re in a virtual meeting, attending a class, or collaborating with your team, TalkTrack works effortlessly to keep track of everything that matters.

AI-Powered Transcription

TalkTrack automatically transcribes speech into text in real-time, so you can focus on the conversation without worrying about note-taking.

Smart Summaries

Using advanced algorithms, TalkTrack summarizes key points, action items, and decisions, making it easier to digest meeting outcomes.

Speaker Identification

TalkTrack intelligently identifies and labels speakers, making it easier to understand who said what during the meeting.

Seamless Collaboration

Share notes, transcripts, and summaries easily with your team for better collaboration and decision-making.
